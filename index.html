Research Goal & Methods

The goal of my research:
improve the intelligence of urban communities facing ever-changing conditions

Computational Social Science

Analysis of social systems in disasters using social media data, including information cascades and influence, community discovery, location aware social networks and evolution, social demographics and inequality.

Applied Statistics & Natural Language Processing

Methods for identifying disaster relief needs and conditions on the ground from social media posts and civilian reporting, including named entity recognition, clustering, classification and representation learning.

Complex Network Modeling & Interference

Development of network-based mathematical techniques for modeling the dynamics of physical infrastructure, including examining flood percolation, phase transition, network adaptation, network resilience and robustness.
